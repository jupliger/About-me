Agro Bayer

The Farmer Experience, also known as Agro Bayer, is a global innersource monorepo site divided into the following regions: Brazil, Argentina, Latam (Latin America except Brazil and Argentina), Canada, United States, and EMEA (Europe, Middle East, and Africa). Being innersource, we build global and local features or adaptations for specific countries. The project itself is the Agro blog, where you can find news, information about Bayer products (seeds and crop protection), communication channels, where to buy from accredited suppliers, and also includes a logged-in area with a points program (Orbia), cashback, technical visit requests, and much more.

Highlights:

Code Refactoring: Led a project to refactor 10,000 lines of code in analytics and data tracking, collaborating with the global team and the American content team. Developed a new standard compatible with new technologies, adopted by 5 more countries.

Recognition: For leading the analytics project, I received the Brazil Innovation Sprint Awards - The Analyzer (Analytics / Metrics) - Most involved data contributor.

International Collaboration: Worked together with global and local teams to ensure solutions meet the specific needs of each region. All work was conducted 100% in English, facilitating communication and collaboration among members from different countries and cultures.
